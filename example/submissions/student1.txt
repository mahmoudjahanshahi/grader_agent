Algorithm to Sort 10 Cards from Smallest to Largest
1. Place all 10 cards face up in a row in front of you.
2. Select the leftmost card. Call it the “current card.”
3. Compare the number on the current card with the number on the card immediately to its right.
4. If the current card is larger than the card to its right, swap their positions.
5. Move one card to the right and make that the new current card.
6. Repeat steps 3–5 until you reach the last card in the row.
7. When you reach the end, return to the first card on the left and repeat the same process.
8. Continue repeating this process until you can go from the first card to the last card without making any swaps.
9. At that point, all cards are arranged from smallest to largest. STOP.
